Hacky Holi

A hackthon




Wishing you a Happy holi ! Akatsuki coding club brings you the first ever hacky holi coding Competition.


Steps to follow

Step-I

Create a team of six members (minimum 1 female member)

Team leader registers the team by filling the goggle form

(Registration fee 30Rs/- per person).


Registration Link : https://forms.gle/TJB3QkMZSdpAYV2LA


Choose a problem statement : https://www.sih.gov.in/sih2022PS

Step-II

Come up with a Solution for the chosen problem statement

Create a Presentation (10-15 slides) for your solution explaining your idea

'Present the solution to the panel of judges.

Teams shortlisted from Round-I move on to Round-II




Step-III


Round II is a 36-hour Coding round where teams create a working prototype of their proposed solution.

Criteria for writing code :

1. Clean code

2.Optimized for Space-Time complexity.



Step-IV


The teams with best implementation of their solution Wins Hacky Holi 2022

Winning teams gets exciting Akatsuki Swags and an opportunity to Represent RCPIT in Smart India Hackathon 2022.
