About Us

Akatsuki is a coding club created by students of TY COMP in 2021,the creation of this club has been in the works eversince from our First
Year in the college and finally with a lot of help and support from Faculty Members and Juniors we were able to make it a reality.
This club aspires to help students across the Institute to become the best versions of themselves and develop their skillset while having a place to consult with their Seniors and Alumni.

MISSION & VISION

"Of the Students For the Student and By the Students"

1.

Our mission is to estabilish a coding culture in our students and inculcate the coding habits necessary to crack Product based companies.

2.

To spark a flame of innovation and positive change in the students, giving them all the necessary tools to tranform their lives and achieve a bright future.

3.

To provide a unique learning environment in a Transdisciplinary and non hierarchical setting.

4.

To eliminate coding phobia from students.
